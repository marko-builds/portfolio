## What to build

Three genuinely distinct hero-section prototypes as throwaway pages on the redesign branch, all
inside the frozen marko tokens: **Linear-restrained** (very dark, mint as the gradient accent,
grain texture, ruthless negative space), **Vercel-monochrome** (grid lines, terminal/code
motifs, sharp contrast), **Raycast-warm** (product-forward, real app screenshots in floating
cards, more color play). Screenshot each with capture-web, run design-critique on each, deliver
the set to Marko. The pick gates everything downstream; prototypes are deleted after the pick.
